Interaction Design

I am a multidisciplinary interaction designer from Nepal, currently based in Rovereto, Italy. With a background in Visual Communication, I engage in participatory and community-based research practices with topics such as financial resilience, alternative economies, experiential learning, e.t.c. through artistic, collaborative and systems thinking methods.
