Challenge

Kalmarhem offers various shared student accommodations, but keeping them clean has been a challenge. We found that the root issue wasnt a lack of rules and regulationsit was a lack of belonging and interaction. Some students saw their stay as temporary, while others faced communication barriers, causing friction.

Results

To bridge this gap, we took a creative approach. We filmed a music video which was integrated with Kalmarhem's welcome email. This was a fun and engaging solution to help new residents connect with older residents and feel at home. By introducing human elements to the existing communication systems, we fostered social bonds and peer support that improved both the living spaces and the experience of sharing them.

Conclusion

We learned that often, the best way to address a problem is by identifying the less obvious underlying cause and tackling it head-on.

Even if the solution seems unorthodox or unrelated at first glance, addressing the root cause can lead to the most effective and lasting impact.
